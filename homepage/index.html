How It Works

A workflow designed for speed and simplicity.

1. Capture Anything, Instantly.

Press a hotkey and your screen dims, ready for selection. The "live" capture feels just like your operating system's native snipping tool. Drag a box over anything—a code snippet, a foreign language comment, a non-selectable error message—and the sidebar instantly appears with your capture.

  • Feels native and responsive, no lag.
  • Select text even from images or videos.
  • Uses blazing-fast native Windows OCR.

2. Extract Text & Unleash AI.

Click "Extract Text" and watch the image turn into selectable text in seconds. From there, the AI is at your command. Need to understand a piece of code? Click "Explain". Replying to a message? Click "Reply". The AI understands the context and gives you exactly what you need.

  • One-click text extraction from any image.
  • Multiple AI actions: Explain, Translate, Reply, Rephrase, and Answer.
  • Seamlessly copy the AI's response or the extracted text.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fluent AI Sidebar free?

Yes, the app is completely free to use. You just need to provide your own Google Gemini API key, which also has a generous free tier.

What platforms are supported?

Currently, the app is officially supported on Windows. macOS and Linux versions are planned. You can contact the author to express interest and get notified when they are available.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Your API key and history are stored locally on your machine. Your captured text is sent directly to the Google Gemini API and is not stored on any third-party servers.

How does the OCR work?

On Windows, we use the powerful and fast native Windows OCR engine. On macOS and Linux, the app uses Tesseract.js, a popular open-source OCR library that runs locally.
